Key Things to Focus on BEFORE Running an Ultramarathon

  1. Mindset Preparation
    • Identify your “WHY” - Your purpose helps you push through the mental and physical challenges during the race.
    • Expect the pain cave - Be prepared for miles of discomfort! It’s a natural part of the race experience.
    • Listen to your body - Learn when to push through discomfort and when to respect injury signs to prevent overexertion.
  2. Physical Training
    • Build a strong base - Consistent running mileage over months to build endurance and prevent injury.
    • Strength training - Focus on glutes, core, and stabilizing muscles to improve overall endurance and running form.
    • Stretching - Incorporate dynamic stretches before running and static stretches afterward to improve flexibility and prevent injuries.
  3. Race-Day Readiness
    • Hydration plan - Understand your sweat rate and electrolyte needs to stay hydrated and maintain energy.
    • Rest & recovery - Prioritize sleep, recovery days, and tapering to ensure peak performance on race day.
